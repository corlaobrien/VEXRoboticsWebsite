Introduction.

Vex Robotics was brought by DELL to Ireland.The idea of it was that young people
could learn and enjoy programming.Soon it became a global product.In Vex
Robotics you design, build and programme.Every year the competition changes.
In 2018 it was a game called "Ringmaster".It was a game where you programme
the robot to pick up rings and place them on poles.

Elements of vex robotics.

2. Programming.
3. Design.
4. Physics.
