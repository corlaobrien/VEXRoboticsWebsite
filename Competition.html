The Competition

On Wednesday 17th of January the Coderdojo team went to CIT to compete in the Vex IQ competition.
The competition started at 8:30and ended at 3:00.
The competition was being comenced in the CIT hall.
There were 3 challenges to do in the arena.

  • Driver Control
  • Autonomous
  • The Teamwork Challenge
    • For the driver control challenge two drivers from each team would enter the ring.The two drivers would have 30 seconds each to get as many points as they can.
    For the autonomous challenge you had to code the robot to pick up the rings on it's own.
    For the teamwork challenge you and another team will play together to get lots of points.
